The Tool

The Leadership Circle Profile

The Leadership Circle Profile is the world's most advanced 360° leadership assessment. It gives your team, your colleagues, and even your boss a structured, anonymous channel to share how they actually experience your leadership.

You assess yourself too. The result is a rich data graphic showing your effectiveness across 30+ dimensions: vision, strategy, relationships, team building, and more. It reveals exactly where your self-perception diverges from reality.

That gap is where the most powerful coaching happens.

“This assessment was one of the most insightful things I’ve ever experienced in my career.”

A sentiment Tania’s clients share, consistently

Anonymous Feedback

Team members respond with complete honesty; their names are never attached to their answers.

30+ Dimensions

Vision, strategy, team building, relationships, purpose, results. All measured and mapped.

The Self-Gap

Actionable Output

The Process

How it works

1

Step 1

Discovery Call

We start with a real conversation about your leadership context, your team dynamics, your challenges and goals. This sets the foundation for a debrief that actually lands.

2

~2 Weeks

The Assessment Goes Out

Your team members receive the anonymous Leadership Circle Profile. Your boss receives a non-anonymous version. You complete your own self-assessment. The data accumulates quietly, candidly.

3

The Debrief

We unpack the data together

We walk through your full Leadership Circle Profile report: your strengths, your blind spots, and the gap between how you see yourself and how your team experiences you. This session alone changes things.

4

Action Plan

You leave with clarity and a concrete path

We translate the data into specific, actionable next steps. You'll know exactly what to change, what to protect, and where your biggest growth opportunity is, right now.
